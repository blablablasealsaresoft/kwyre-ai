Capabilities

Everything a Research Team Needs

From literature review to publication-ready drafts — LabMind covers the full research lifecycle.

🔍

Literature Synthesis

Semantic search over your corpus with FAISS + sentence-transformers. Synthesize multiple papers into thematic summaries, consensus points, and research gaps.

🧪

Experiment Design

Generate structured protocols with design selection (RCT, factorial, crossover), power-analysis-driven sample sizing, controls, and confound identification.

💡

Hypothesis Generation

Transform observations into testable hypotheses with null/alternative formulations, type classification, and suggested study designs.

📊

Statistical Planning

Describe your data and get the right test. Covers t-tests, ANOVA, chi-square, Mann-Whitney, Kruskal-Wallis, regression — with assumptions checklists.

📝

Paper Drafting

Scaffold publication-ready sections — introduction, methods, results, discussion — with APA-aligned templates and writing guidance.

🔗

Citation Graph Analysis

Map document relationships, identify seminal works, trace research lineages, and surface cross-disciplinary connections in your literature corpus.

Developer-first

One API, Full Research Stack

Clean REST endpoints. JSON in, JSON out. Drop into any pipeline.

# Search literature by meaning, not keywords curl -X POST http://localhost:8000/v1/literature/search \ -H "Content-Type: application/json" \ -d '{ "query": "CRISPR off-target effects in vivo", "k": 5 }' # Design an experiment with power analysis curl -X POST http://localhost:8000/v1/experiment/design \ -H "Content-Type: application/json" \ -d '{ "research_question": "Does compound X reduce tumor growth?", "independent_vars": ["compound_X_dose"], "dependent_vars": ["tumor_volume"], "hypothesis": "Compound X reduces tumor volume", "expected_effect_size": 0.6 }'

Endpoints

Built on FastAPI with automatic OpenAPI docs at /docs.

  • POST /v1/literature/search — semantic similarity
  • POST /v1/literature/synthesize — multi-paper synthesis
  • POST /v1/experiment/design — protocol generation
  • POST /v1/hypothesis/generate — pattern-based hypotheses
  • POST /v1/stats/plan — statistical test recommender
  • POST /v1/paper/draft — section scaffolding
