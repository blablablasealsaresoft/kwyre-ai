Analysis Capabilities

Six Analysis Modes

Each mode is purpose-built for a specific phase of game preparation and execution. Every analysis runs through Kwyre's local AI — your data never leaves your machine.

📋

Pre-Game Scouting Report

Full matchup breakdown with offensive tendencies, defensive scheme analysis, head-to-head advantages, and situational strategy for red zone, 3rd down, and 2-minute drills.

🎯

Situational Play Call

Input down, distance, field position, score, and defensive alignment. Get the optimal play call with primary read, hot read, EV estimate, and two alternatives.

🔍

Blitz & Coverage Read

Predict blitz probability, coverage shell, pressure type, individual matchup assignments, and where the vulnerability lies for the offense to exploit.

🏃

Player Movement Profile

Deep dive on any player's route tree, tendencies by down and distance, red zone behavior, injury impact on movement, and defensive containment strategy.

📖

Playbook Reverse Engineer

Reconstruct a team's offensive scheme from formation frequencies, motion patterns, tendency-breakers, and how the playbook has evolved over 5 seasons.

📊

Post-Game Breakdown

Analyze completed games with drive-by-drive critical sequence analysis, scheme evaluation, key matchups, and what each team should adjust.

🏈 Live Game Mode

Connect to a real-time game feed via WebSocket. The AI tracks score, clock, down, distance, field position, and possession — automatically generating play suggestions as the game unfolds. Demo mode simulates a full game for testing.
