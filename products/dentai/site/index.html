Capabilities

Everything your practice needs

From first exam to final restoration, DentAI assists at every clinical and administrative touchpoint.

📋

Treatment Planning

Enter symptoms, history, and findings. Receive a phased treatment plan with urgent, necessary, and elective procedures — each with estimated costs, durations, and clinical rationale.

🖼

Radiograph Analysis Prep

Upload dental radiographs for structured pre-analysis. Images are stored securely and queued for AI-assisted findings once diagnostic imaging integration is live.

💳

CDT Insurance Coding

Describe any dental procedure in plain language. DentAI returns the top matching CDT codes with confidence scores, descriptions, and category — reducing claim denials.

📝

Clinical SOAP Notes

Generate compliant SOAP-format clinical notes from structured input. Includes subjective, objective, assessment, and plan sections with proper dental terminology.

⚠

Procedure Risk Assessment

Score procedural risk based on patient factors — age, medications, systemic conditions. Receive clear risk levels, contributing factors, and clinical recommendations.

🔬

Material Recommendations

Get material options for crowns, fillings, implants, and more — with pros, cons, cost ranges, longevity estimates, and best-use guidance for each option.

🔒

HIPAA-Ready Architecture

DentAI is designed with healthcare data security in mind. Patient data is processed in-memory with no persistent storage of PHI. Deploy on-premises or in a HIPAA-eligible cloud environment for full compliance. BAA available for enterprise customers.
